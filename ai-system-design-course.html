Course Philosophy
System design is not about memorizing architectures — it's about reasoning through trade-offs under constraints. Every week teaches you a mental model, not a blueprint. By Week 8 you should be able to walk into any system design conversation, draw a reasonable starting architecture, and ask the right questions to refine it. Your H&M GenAI control plane is the running case study — every week reframes what you built against new system design lenses.
